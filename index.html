The Logic Workshop

The Logic Workshop (TLW) is an ed-tech company specializing in the creation of high-quality, on-demand educational content tailored specifically for educational institutions. Founded with a clear mission to redefine educational content development.TLW offers a diverse array of content types that include simulators, articles, animations, games, and even movies. Think of us as your one-stop-shop for all things educational content. We're not just here to create content; we're here to create experiences. Simulators for hands-on learning, articles to expand knowledge, animations to visualize complex concepts, games to make learning fun, and movies to captivate and educate – we've got it all covered.

Hey there! I'm Manan, a curious 18-year-old on a journey of exploration and creation. As the founder of the Logic Workshop, I'm passionate about sharing the wonders of technology and problem-solving with fellow enthusiasts.
